About Us

Our Story

Finwizz Consultants Pvt. Ltd (FCPL), incorporated in the year 2005 is the brainchild of three seasoned professionals who came together from background of Banking Industry. One with decade plus experience in banking & top corporates, the second a finance & marketing expert & the third a highly skilled Engineer.

Their extensive & diverse professional experience & expertise brought them together for a common vision. To build an organization that successfully bridges the gap & provides a complete solution from customer need to the solution design in a fast, simple & safe approach.

Finwizz Today

Today, Finwizz is successful & en route to accomplishing its vision which started a decade ago. While continuing to cruise through the path laid towards being the largest financial solutions provider for banking institution & individuals across Pan India. Finwizz today stands at the Pinnacle of presence, with vast spread offices across 30+ cities including the capital & major metros.

The Enhanced Vision

We are now hungry to take the processing time & customer experience a notch higher & set new standards in our space.

Our vision is now to be the first & largest consultant providing fastest, simplest, safest & most consistent financial solution to our customers. We are now, in the process of delivering our customers with service & fulfillment within 300 seconds of their request. We are confident & believe that with over a decade of experience in financial domain & with the help of our well balanced Offline & Online teams, the day for our enhanced vision to be met is a touch away. With our 800+ family and growing every minute , we are an organization and a team here to stay & change the way world looks at our domain today.

Finwizz has ventured into the technology space and is called Finwizz Digital. Finwizz Digital provides IT based solutions from website’s, mobile app’s building, 360 CRM, GPS based solutions & much more.

Finwizz is also into hospitality services. The Hospitality Services has got the franchise rights from Cream Centre; a renowned name in multi cuisine specialty restaurant to runs its unit in Ahmedabad & is the largest of its kind.
