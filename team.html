Our Team



Full Name: Rajeev Singhvi

Designation: Owner & Director

Life’s Mantra/Tag Line: Strive not to be a success, but rather to be of value

A Law Graduate, started his professional journey with Escorts Finance at Jaipur, then moved on to Apple Finance & then to Transamerica Finance. His last endeavor was with ICICI Bank as Regional Manger- Personal Loans based out of Mumbai.

With over 12 years of experience in various domains such as Technology, Finance & Banking, he took a leap of faith to pursue his dream & decided to make his own destiny.

On the professional journey with ICICI Bank he met two individuals envisaging similar dreams to make something Big. Today Rajeev is a proud co-owner of Finwizz Management Consultants, the largest retail financial products distribution company in India. The Company is all set to expand its wings thru digitalizing the business processes.

His key skills include Finance, Accounts, Man management and Operation management. When he is away from one passion he fulfills his other passions for Tennis, off roading, travelling and spending time with family. He lives in Ahmedabad.



Full Name: Anil Mathew

Designation: Owner & Director

Life’s Mantra/Tag Line: Brave hearts and clear eyes, can never lose

Having developed a passion for financial products at a very young age, Anil moved to the financial Capital of Mumbai after completing his MBA in Finance & Marketing from Bharathiar University, Coimbatore. He started his career with an international broking unit of the Chicago based Cargill Investor Services .Later he joined ICICI Bank in its unsecured business division. His last assignment was with Citibank NA in its Mortgage Business.

The huge underlying potential in the retail lending segment paved way to explore business opportunity along with couple of his colleagues who shared the same vision and by the end of 2005 Brand Finwizz was born.

Today the Company is the leader in unsecured business and is growing steadily. The Company has set very high benchmarks by adopting new technology to meet new challenges.

His key skills include Relationship Management, Portfolio Management, Retail Banking & Loans.

His other hobbies include playing lawn tennis, badminton & watching cricket, spending time with family & friends. He lives in Pune.



Full Name: Syed Haseeb

Designation: Owner & Director

Life’s Mantra/Tag Line: Work is worship, it’s now or never

Brought up in a humble family in Kolar Gold Field (KGF), a kid with bright eyes and dreams in his eyes set out to change the course of his life. The first step was when he got shortlisted in one of the well-known engineering college, National Institute of Technology Karnataka, Surathkal. Followed by a placement with Chennai based diversified conglomerate Murugappa Group. His next endeavor was with ICICI bank as a manager in its Personal Loans Business.

In ICICI bank he was bitten by the entrepreneurship zeal and came together with two more like minded & experienced individual to form the today’s largest financial products distribution company Finwizz.

His key skills are Sales, business development and is known for team building & employee relations. When he is away from office and laptop, he is busy playing cricket, football and games with his Son. Likes river rafting,Water Sports and beaches. He lives in Mumbai.
